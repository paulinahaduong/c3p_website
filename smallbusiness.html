Small Business Websites

An animation by Robert, Sagyan, and Adil

This is a project created by Robert, Sagyan, and Adil focusing on the lack of small businesses with an online presence. Our Scratch project includes a consumer looking for a restaurant's website but failing to do so because it does not have one. As a result the customer goes to a McDonalds and the small business owner is clearly upset because he has very little business.
