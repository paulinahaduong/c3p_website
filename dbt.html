A Day in the Life

A game by Kayden, Kelly, Zenry, and Ella

Our game was produced so that people can learn about mental health through an interesting story line. Even though it is an unfinished project, we still got pretty far and we think some people can get a better understanding of the struggles of living with a mental disability. We hope people will go through this visual novel and think about their own lives - privilege they may have, what other people are going through, and not taking their bodies for granted.
