A Cambridge Mayor's Summer Youth Employment site

In summer 2017, Cambridge teens (ages 14-18) explored their own ideas and created projects about what civic engagement means to them. These projects took the form of interactive games about a particular issue, multimedia oral histories, and more! Check out some of their project prototypes below.
